Qzzo Technologies

SERVICES

Design, UI/UX Design & App Development, Iot Systems, Web Development, Django Backend, Deep Learning

Summary

One of our clients, Qzzo technologies provide safe and comfortable school vehicle service for children. They had the vision to ensure that the parents’ life becomes stress-free as they can easily track their children by just one click on an app. Thus our task here was to turn their vision into a reality by providing them our all round holistic services, so that they could get the solutions to all their requirements under a singular platform.

The services that we provided them were Design, UI/UX Design & App Development, IOT Systems, Web Development, Django Backend Development and Deep Learning.

Design

Our Design Team worked diligiently to not only work towards the aesthetic of the company but also to give them an identity that fell into place with the company's ideals. We picked ORANGE as the brand colour, since the colour orange invokes the feeling of excitement, enthusiasm and warmth, something that the company tried to give to the children who were travelling from their homes to their schools.
